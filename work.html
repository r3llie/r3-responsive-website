& Why

Being an autistic artist means I am exceptionally creative & detail oriented in my area of focussed interests; those of which usually stay the same. I am what they call a hyposensative (sensory seeking) autistic, which means I thrive in most high-pressure environments. Autism is not an illness/disease caused by a toxin or vaccine. It's a neurotype that continues to be disabling for many due to society's unwillingness to view our needs as human.
