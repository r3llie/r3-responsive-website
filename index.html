Music

Even though I started writing & producing my own songs about 5 years ago, but music has always felt like therapy. My sound is influenced by my life experiences as a classical dancer & as a late-diagnosed autistic female. Since my diagnosis; I've been using my little closet home recording studio to connect with similar audiences. And for me, being a musician isn't just about being vocal or expressing a vibe ~it's about listening too.

Modeling

Even though I started walking for high-end fashion companies about 15 years ago; the stage has always felt like home. It's the one place where I have an excuse to be "weird", where I have an excuse to be me. On the daily, you can usually find me dressed in comfy-chic clothing with natural makeup. And for me, being a model isn't just about being pretty or repping a brand ~it's about transparency too.

Advocacy

Even though I started prioritizing my own mental health about 2 years ago; better late than never is what I say. ~Being autistic & not knowing was a nightmare!
