Music

Even though I started writing & producing my own songs just 5 years ago; music has always been my therapy. My unique sound is influenced by my life experience as a classical dancer & as a late-diagnosed autistic female. Since my diagnosis; I've been using my little closet home recording studio to connect more openly with similar audiences. And for me, being a musician isn't just about being vocal or expressing a vibe ~it's about listening too.

Modeling

Even though I started walking for high-end clothing companies some 15 years ago; being center stage still feels like home to me. It's the one place where I have an excuse to be "weird", where I have an excuse to be me. On the daily, you can usually find me dressed in comfy-chic fashion. And for me, being a model isn't just about being pretty or repping a brand ~it's about transparency too.

Advocacy

Even though I started prioritizing my own mental health later in life, better late than never is what I say. Today, I'm so grateful to know how my neurodivergent brain works. Now that I have the correct tools to be sucessful in life; I can fight for the little one who couldn't ask questions. I can fight for the little one who was forced to hide. And for me, being an advocated isn't just about standing up for what's right or being brave ~it's about education too.
