If you haven't already noticed; how I function in the world is a huge part of my identity. But it wasn't always this way. It all started with one google search on a word I had never heard before, "aspergers". And as much as I have grown to the dislike this "high functioning" label, it still acts as a bridge for people like me.

Being an autistic artist means I am exceptionally creative & detail oriented in my area of focussed intrests; those of which usually stay the same. I am also what they consider a hyposensative (sensory keeking) autistic, which means I thrive in most high-pressure environments. My work is to counteract the belief that Autism is an illness/disease caused by a toxin or vaccine. What is "it" then you may ask? It's a neurotype that continues to be disabling for many due to society's unwillingness to view our needs as human.
